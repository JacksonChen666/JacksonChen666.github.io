To get the last interactions of the people you follow, please login in to twitter

The program will immediately start getting the last interactions after logging in, and may take up to a minute. Please be patient.

Q&A:
Q: What data does this program collect?
A: All tweets, retweets, liked tweets from your profile (max depends on twitter's limits), and all direct messages (twitter limits within 30 days). Only the dates and the user's name and username are pulled from those information, and not kept for longer than needed (start-end processing).

Q: Twitter says it can do a lot of things. Why does it do all of that?
A: I cannot specify which specific permissions should be allowed, so I can only do the most which is the best possible for the program.
